How to Play

🎹 Theremin Mode

  • Move hand UP/DOWN to control pitch (higher = higher pitch)
  • Move hand LEFT/RIGHT to control volume (right = louder)
  • Open/close hand to adjust filter brightness
  • Use second hand for modulation effects

🎼 Keyboard Mode

  • Move hand LEFT/RIGHT to select musical notes
  • OPEN hand to play the selected note
  • CLOSE hand to stop playing
  • Vertical position controls volume

🎨 Experimental Mode

  • Diagonal movements create unique sounds
  • Distance between two hands controls reverb
  • Finger spread adds vibrato effect
  • Explore and find your own patterns!
